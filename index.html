About us

From code to canvas

We're Richard and Jared, the co-founders of Twist, and we are thrilled to partner with you on your journey.

In a not-so-distant past, we were navigating the intricacies of Silicon Valley, leading Pied Piper through the tech landscape's ups and downs. After successfully exiting our startup, we found ourselves yearning for a new challenge, a fresh canvas where our creativity could flourish in a different realm.

Enter Twist, our brainchild and a testament to our belief in the transformative power of design. We transitioned from the world of algorithms and lines of code to the vibrant and visually compelling universe of design. Embracing this change, we've embarked on a mission to help businesses like yours elevate their brands through exceptional design solutions.

Our design subscription agency offers more than just graphics; it's a partnership focused on unlimited possibilities. With one simple subscription fee, you gain access to a dedicated team of skilled designers ready to bring your vision to life. Whether it's crafting a compelling logo, refining your brand identity, or producing eye-catching marketing materials - we've got you covered.

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Some answers to some Frequently asked questions

How quickly can Twist deliver designs for our business?

Twist is designed for speed! Our subscription model ensures a quick turnaround time, typically delivering initial design concepts within a few days. The collaborative approach between our team and clients facilitates efficient communication, ensuring your designs are ready when you need them.

How can i request revision to the designs provided?

Absolutely! Your satisfaction is our priority. Our subscription includes unlimited revisions, allowing you to fine-tune and perfect the designs until they align perfectly with your vision. We believe in collaboration, ensuring the end result exceeds your expectations.

How does Twist determine its pricing?

Our pricing is straightforward and transparent. We offer a simple subscription fee that covers unlimited designs, revisions, and our dedicated design team's expertise. No hidden costs or surprises - just a clear, affordable pricing structure designed to meet your business needs.

What type of designs does Twist specialize in?

Twist is your one-stop destination for a variety of designs. Whether you need a striking logo, engaging marketing materials, or a cohesive brand identity, we've got you covered. Our diverse team of designers is skilled in creating visuals that resonate with your target audience across different platforms.

Can Twist help in ongoing design needs for my business

Absolutely! Twist is not just about one-off designs; we're your long-term design partner. Our subscription model is designed to accommodate ongoing design needs, ensuring your brand maintains a consistent and compelling visual presence as your business evolves. Consider us an extension of your team, dedicated to supporting your design requirements over the long haul.
